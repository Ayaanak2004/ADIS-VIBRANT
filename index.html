Our services

Support that feels like everyday life — just better.

We offer a range of services designed to support NDIS participants in building skills, independence and confidence while still having fun.

Sports & recreational activity

From casual backyard games to structured sports sessions, we help participants stay active, build coordination and enjoy being part of a team.

Physical confidence Motor skills Team play

Gaming (digital & board)

Safe, guided gaming sessions to build social skills, problem solving and focus — using both video games and classic board games.

Social connection Thinking skills Turn-taking

Tutoring (English & Maths)

Gentle, patient tutoring to support schoolwork, homework and basic skills in English and Maths, tailored to each learner.

Homework help Confidence building Foundational skills

Community care & socialising

Gentle support to get out into the community — catching up with friends, visiting local spots and practising social skills.

Community access Social skills Confidence

Leisure: long drives & nature

Relaxing drives, beach visits and nature walks to reset, explore and enjoy calm, sensory-friendly outings.

Nature breaks Stress relief New experiences

Necessities & daily life

Practical support with shopping and other essentials — building independence with everyday tasks at your pace.

Shopping support Life skills Routine building
