Who we are

ADIS SUPPORT — here to back your goals, big and small.

ADIS SUPPORT is a community-minded support provider focused on creating meaningful, enjoyable experiences for people living with disability. We believe support should feel safe, fun and empowering — not clinical or intimidating.

We take the time to understand each participant, their interests and their routine. From there, we shape programs that build skills, confidence and independence: whether that's mastering a new game, joining a sports activity, keeping up with school work, or simply getting out to explore the community.

Our approach is simple: respect, consistency and genuine care. We support individuals, but we also walk alongside families and carers so that everyone feels informed and involved.
